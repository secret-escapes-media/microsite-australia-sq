From buzzing, urban landscapes packed with artisanal foodie delights and world-leading cultural exploits, to irresistible countryside framed by mountain vistas and lakeside splendour, this prize is set to offer you the finest, curated Australian getaway.

10 nights of opulent digs and chic retreats await, with Avis car hire allowing you to really adventure into the glorious landscapes you’ll encounter – all brought to you by Trailfinders, the UK’s No.1 Tour Operator Down Under and Singapore Airlines.

Start your luxurious getaway with flights from Singapore Airlines in their Premium Economy Class. Touch down in Melbourne to begin your adventure...
