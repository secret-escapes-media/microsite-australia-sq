Begin your Australian adventure in affordable luxury; fly Singapore Airlines.

What better way to travel to the golden shores and urban jungles of Australia than with the world’s most awarded airline?

Singapore Airlines has been serving Australia for more than 50 years, remaining the country’s most frequent visitor. With over 150 flights on its weekly schedule, including four daily from London and five weekly from Manchester, travel in comfort from the UK to nine Australian cities. Choose a regional departure with partners Flybe, National Express coaches and Great Western Railway and once you’ve arrived, continue your journey with Virgin Australia, offering 3,000 weekly flights to over 40 countrywide destinations.

View all flights features: - title: Modern Fleet content: >

With a fleet of modern aircraft, Singapore Airlines offer premium in-flight comfort at an affordable price. Its newly unveiled cabins offer more space and privacy across all classes, as well as exceptional dining opportunities, designed by world-renowned exerts, and bespoke on-board elements created with the Singapore Airlines’ customer in mind.

- title: Changi Airport content: >

With complimentary stop-overs in the award-winning Changi Airport, Singapore Airlines’ guests can enjoy a pleasant, seamless transfer to Australia. While you’re there, you won’t be short on things to do, with extensive shopping and dining facilities, free WiFi, a swimming pool and cinema, and the world’s first airport-based butterfly garden. All Singapore Airlines passengers will receive a S$20 (£11.50) voucher to spend in Changi during their transfer.

- title: Rewards content: >

Singapore Airlines also offer its frequent flyers a special rewards scheme. KrisFlyer allows regular guests to earn and burn miles with Virgin Atlantic, Virgin Australia and all Star Alliance airlines, while Elite Gold members can access over 1,000 international Star Alliance lounges, including Virgin Australia Lounges throughout Australia.

- title: Exceptional Services content: >

Boasting exceptional service, the world’s most awarded airline is renowned for going the extra mile when it comes to its customer. As well as hotel and transport deals to complete your holiday down under, Singapore Airlines’ well-timed flights minimize the effects of jet lag, meaning your adventure can begin as soon as you step off the plane.
