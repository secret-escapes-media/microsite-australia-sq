Begin your Australian adventure in affordable luxury; fly Singapore Airlines.

What better way to travel to the golden shores and urban jungles of Australia than with the world’s most awarded airline?

Singapore Airlines has been serving Australia for more than 50 years, remaining the country’s most frequent visitor with over 150 flights per week to nine Australian cities. Four daily flights from Heathrow and five weekly from Manchester make travelling from the UK a breeze. UK Regional departures are available with partners Flybe, National Express and Great Western Railway. Fly into Singapore’s Changi Airport for your transfer, with the option to stopover and explore the city. Once you’ve arrived, continue your journey with partners Virgin Australia, offering 3,000 weekly flights to over 40 country wide destinations.

Singapore Airlines offers flights to nine Australian gateways – Brisbane, the Gold Coast*, Sydney, Melbourne, Adelaide, Perth, Darwin**, Cairns** and Canberra. Operated by Singapore Airlines subsidiaries *Scoot and **SilkAir.

- title: Fly a modern fleet id: modern favourite: true content: >

With a fleet of modern aircraft, Singapore Airlines offer premium in-flight comfort at an affordable price. As of 17 February, the new A380 will complete the London to Singapore route, offering innovative interiors across all classes. Each has been upgraded for extra comfort and luxury. The A350 service retains the comfort and convenience of a Singapore Airlines flight, with exceptional dining opportunities and bespoke on-board elements created with the Singapore Airlines’ customer in mind.

- title: Economy Class id: economy content: >

Economy Class is set to let you travel in comfort, with seats designed to give you more personal space, even if the seat in front of you is reclined. If you want more legroom, you can select Preferred Seats. With a wider seat pitch and a location near the exits it’s in a prime position. KrisWorld entertainment completes the experience.

- title: Premium Economy id: premium content: >

In exclusive, spacious, 2-4-2 cabin, Premium Economy Class seats are designed with a seat pitch of 38 inches for your enhanced comfort. Add that to the extra choices such as champagne, the possibility to pre-book your meal with the airline’s Book the Cook programme, plus exclusive privileges – all delivered by an award-winning service – Premium Economy is worth the upgrade.

- title: Business Class id: business content: >

Let everything go to plan with Business Class. Reserve your meal with Book the Cook and enjoy uninterrupted sleep on your fully-flat bed. Don’t leave it to chance – wake-up refreshed, ready to complete your business after a rejuvenating service with Singapore Airlines.

- title: First Class & Suites id: first-class content: >

Relish in the opulence that First Class aboard a Singapore Airlines flight affords. Breakfast in bed, no matter what the time. Whether you need a quiet space to work or somewhere to relax, enjoy sumptuous and delicately prepared meals. If you simply want to enjoy a film, the lie-flat beds make everything possible… Take it one step further with a whole suite to yourself for ultimate privacy and personal space when you’re flying on the A380.

- title: Complimentary Privileges at Award Winning Changi Airport id: changi content: >

Travelling via Singapore’s award-winning Changi Airport, Singapore Airlines’ guests can enjoy a pleasant, seamless transfer to Australia. While you’re there, you won’t be short on things to do, with extensive shopping and dining facilities, a swimming pool, free WiFi and cinema, and the world’s first airport-based butterfly garden. All Singapore Airlines passengers will receive a S$20 (£11.50) voucher to spend in Changi during their transfer (on either way of travel).

- title: KrisFlyer Rewards & Benefits id: krisflyer content: >

Singapore Airlines also offer its frequent flyers a special rewards scheme. KrisFlyer allows regular guests to earn and burn miles with Singapore Airlines, Virgin Atlantic, Virgin Australia and all Star Alliance airlines, while Elite Gold members can access over 1,000 international Star Alliance lounges, including Virgin Australia Lounges throughout Australia.
